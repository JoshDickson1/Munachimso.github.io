The Christmas outreach to Citadel of Mission Int'l Orphanage Home held on 25th December 2025 in Lagos was a heartwarming and impactful success.

About This Event

This Christmas outreach was organized to bring love, care, and essential support to the children of Citadel of Mission Int'l Orphanage Home in Lagos, Nigeria. The initiative focused on sharing joy during the festive season while addressing basic needs within the orphanage.

The children received foodstuffs, toiletries, and stationery items to support their daily living and educational needs. Beyond material support, the outreach created a warm environment of connection, celebration, and emotional encouragement.

This event reflects a continued commitment to uplifting vulnerable children and reinforcing the belief that compassion, when shared intentionally, can create lasting impact.

Our Impact

  • Touched the lives of approximately 30 children
  • Distributed foodstuffs to support daily nutrition
  • Provided toiletries to promote hygiene and wellbeing
  • Supplied stationery items to encourage education
  • Created a joyful and memorable Christmas experience
