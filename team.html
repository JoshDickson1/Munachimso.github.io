About the Founder

Alexandra Tochi Chibueze

Founder & CEO, Alexaz Care Foundation

Alexandra Chibueze is a passionate humanitarian, corporate leader, and the Founder of Alexaz Care Foundation. A Bachelor in Science graduate and a Master's degree holder in Business Administration, Alexandra began her charity work during her national service, visiting orphanages and schools to support less privileged children.

While serving under the Nigerian Navy, her outstanding commitment to service and community outreach earned her recognition from the Nigerian government, who subsequently Appointed her to manage the operations of an indigenous airline. Today, as the head of Alexaz Care Foundation, Alexandra continues to champion sustainable development initiatives across Nigeria.

"True leadership begins with service, and lasting impact is achieved when compassion meets action."

Through her dynamic leadership, Alexaz Care Foundation has become a beacon of hope, uniting volunteers, donors, and institutions to create lasting change in communities that need it most. Alexandra remains committed to building a future where empathy, empowerment, and equality form the foundation of progress.
