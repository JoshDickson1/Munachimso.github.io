Alexaz Care Foundation successfully carried out our community health outreach at the Mother and Child Care Hospital, Ogombo.

About This Event

The outreach was a fulfilling and impactful experience, as we were warmly received by the hospital management and staff. Their cooperation and support made the entire process smooth and well-coordinated.

We worked closely with the hospital's accounts team to facilitate the subsidy process, ensuring that selected patients received the intended financial support.

Most importantly, we were able to put smiles on the faces of mothers and families, easing their burden and bringing hope during a critical time.

It was truly a rewarding experience, and we remain committed to continuing our mission of service, compassion, and community impact.

Our Impact

  • Provided financial relief through healthcare subsidies
  • Supported mothers and families during critical medical care
  • Collaborated effectively with hospital management and staff
  • Ensured smooth coordination with the hospital accounts team
  • Delivered hope, care, and emotional support to patients
