The Alexaz Care Foundation successfully carried out a Christmas outreach at Citadel of Mission Int'l Orphanage Home on 25th December, 2025 in Lagos, Nigeria.

About This Event

As part of its Christmas outreach initiatives, The Alexaz Care Foundation visited the Citadel of Mission International Orphanage Home on 25th December, 2025, to share love, care, and support with children during the festive season.

The outreach was aimed at creating moments of joy and reassurance for the children, reminding them that they are seen, valued, and supported. Through meaningful interaction and thoughtful giving, the Foundation sought to uplift spirits and contribute positively to their overall wellbeing.

This Christmas outreach reflects the Foundation‚Äôs commitment to compassion-driven impact, especially for vulnerable children. By reaching out during a season centered on love and generosity, the initiative reinforced the importance of community, empathy, and shared responsibility.

Our Impact

  • Reached and supported approximately 30 children
  • Distributed foodstuffs to support daily nutrition
  • Provided toiletries to promote personal hygiene
  • Donated stationery materials to encourage learning and education
  • Created a joyful and memorable Christmas experience for the children
