Alexaz Care Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing humanitarian aid and relief to less privileged individuals and communities.

Our focus extends beyond immediate relief ‚Äî we also promote awareness initiatives that address critical social and health issues, while fostering partnerships with donors, organizations, and volunteers to maximize impact and outreach.
