About This Event

The Alexaz Care Foundation proudly partnered with the Golf & Tour Charity Association to host the 13th edition of the EKO Tournament at the prestigious Dolphin Golf Club in Lagos. This landmark event brought together community leaders, philanthropists, and military families for a day of unity, celebration, and meaningful charitable impact.

The tournament served as more than just a sporting event‚Äîit was a powerful demonstration of community solidarity and commitment to supporting those who serve our nation. Through generous contributions from participants and sponsors, the foundation was able to distribute 200 bags of rice and cash donations, providing essential nutritional support and demonstrating our deep appreciation for their service and sacrifice.

This collaborative outreach exemplifies the core mission of Alexaz Care Foundation: to create sustainable impact through strategic partnerships and community-driven initiatives. The success of this event highlights the power of collective action in addressing the needs of vulnerable populations and strengthening the fabric of our society.

Our Impact

  • Distributed 200 bags of rice to military families in need
  • Strengthened community bonds through collaborative partnership
  • Provided nutritional support for over 200 families
  • Raised awareness about the needs of military families
  • Created a sustainable model for future charitable golf tournaments
