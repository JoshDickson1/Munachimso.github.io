The Beginning

In 2019, during National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) year in Calabar, Nigeria, Alexandra embarked on what began as a personal community development project; one that would later become the foundation of Alexaz Care Foundation.

With the generous support of the Nigerian Navy, we reached out to the beautiful children of Uwanse Motherless Home, providing food, relief materials, and educational support.

Standing among those children, she realized that real change begins when we decide to lend a helping hand, no matter how small the act may seem. That day, a vision was born; to build a platform dedicated to spreading kindness, empowering others, and touching lives across communities.

Today, that dream continues to grow through the work of Alexaz Care Foundation, as we stay true to our mission of creating lasting impact‚Ä¶ one life, one act of care at a time.
